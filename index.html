How to make Oreo Cheesecake

Today we're going to make Oreo Cheesecake. Oreo Cheesecake that we're going to make consist three layers. The first layer is the Oreo crust, second layer is the vanilla filling and the last layer is the chocolate filling.

INGREDIENTS

Crust

  • 2 packs of Oreo (chrused)
  • 2 tbs Butter, melted (salted or unsalted is fine)

Filling A (Vanilla with Cheese)

  • 1/2 block of Cheedar Cheese
  • 700 mL of Milk
  • 2 tbs Cornstarch
  • 2 tsp Gelatin
  • 2 tbs Sweetened Condensed Milk

Filling B (Chocolate)

  • 4 small blocks of Dark Chocolate
  • 700 mL of Chocolate Milk
  • 1 tbs Cornstarch
  • 2 tbs Cocoa Powder
